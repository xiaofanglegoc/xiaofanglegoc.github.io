About Me

Hey! I am currently post-doc at LIRIS (UMR 5205 CNRS), École Centrale de Lyon, France. Right now, I am working with Prof. Liming Chen and Dr. Emmanuel Dellandréa on FUI project PIKAFLEX . My current resarch is on 3D CAD object recogntion and retievial.

I am also work on an active research topic in machine learning, the transfer learning, especially on domain adaptation

During My PhD studies, I worked on the unsupervised image segmentation and multiple objects tracking with Prof. Simon Masnou and Prof. Liming Chen. I completed my M.E. and B.S. majored in Biomedical Engineering in School of Geosciences and Info-physics from Central South University, Changsha, China. I was working on the medical image processing, liver CT image segmentation with Prof. Yuqian Zhao

My research interests focus on 2D/3D image understanding including image segmentation, image classification, object detection; machine learning, especially deep learning and transfer learning; robotic vision.
